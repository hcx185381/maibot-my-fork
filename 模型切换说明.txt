========================================
     MaiBot Model Switch Guide
========================================

NEW SCRIPTS (English version to avoid encoding issues):

1. switch_to_glm.bat
   - Switch to GLM models
   - Supports emoji/image recognition
   - Best for: Daily chat, emoji interaction

2. switch_to_deepseek_v3.bat
   - Switch to DeepSeek-V3 (Fast)
   - NO emoji/image recognition
   - Best for: Programming, quick questions

3. switch_to_deepseek_r1.bat
   - Switch to DeepSeek-R1 (Thinking)
   - NO emoji/image recognition
   - Best for: Math, logic, reasoning

========================================
HOW TO USE:
========================================

Double-click the .bat file to switch models

The script will:
1. Backup your config
2. Switch models
3. Restart container
4. Done in 10-20 seconds

========================================
MODEL DETAILS:
========================================

GLM Models (switch_to_glm.bat)
  - Reply: glm-4-plus
  - Plan: glm-4-plus
  - Vision: glm-4v-plus
  - Utils: glm-4-air
  - Emoji recognition: YES
  - Image recognition: YES

DeepSeek-V3 (switch_to_deepseek_v3.bat)
  - All tasks: deepseek-chat
  - Emoji recognition: NO
  - Image recognition: NO
  - Speed: Fast
  - Best for: Programming, chat

DeepSeek-R1 (switch_to_deepseek_r1.bat)
  - All tasks: deepseek-reasoner
  - Emoji recognition: NO
  - Image recognition: NO
  - Speed: Slow (needs thinking time)
  - Best for: Math, logic, reasoning
